Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Information We Collect

Horse Racer AI is a simulation and entertainment app. We collect minimal data:

  • App usage statistics to improve features
  • Device information for crash reports
  • Optional: Your race predictions and simulation settings

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve app performance and features
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Enhance AI prediction accuracy (using anonymized data only)

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. Simulation results and predictions are stored locally on your device.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your stored data anytime in app settings
  • Opt out of anonymous usage statistics
  • Request your data report via support@horseracerai.com

Children's Privacy

Horse Racer AI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of significant changes via app update notes or email.

Questions about privacy? Email: privacy@horseracerai.com
